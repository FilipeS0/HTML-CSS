History of Android Pet

Probably you know that the OS Android, keep by Google is one of the most used to mobile devices on all the world. But maybe you don't know that your pet and your name has a really Curiosity Story. Then follow this article to learn a lot of things about this little robot

The First Version

The fist try to build a pet has started in 2007 and came from a developer known as Dan Morrill. He tells that he opened the lnkscape (Free Software to vetorization of images) and created your own robot version. His objectiv was only personificate the system just for his team, there wasn't any team request to make a pet.

This first version was even named in honor of its creator

A new mascots emerge

The idea of to have a mascot was maturing and the mission was passed by a professional in the field. The ilustrator Russa Irina Block also Google employee, had the mission of represent the small robot of a pleasant way.

The main idea of Irinia was represnt all graphicly with a few traces and a flat way. The design should also create a sense of identification with the viewer. Then emerged the Bugdroid, the new Android mascot

The main inspiration to the new Bugdroid traces comes from those little illustrations on the bathrooms door to indicate each gender of each door. Tell it the legend that the artist was making on her table in the Googles office and looked to the side and the identification was immediatly: easy, clean, objective.
